Our Story

This generations of
silk & craftsmanship

Tharan Boutique began in 2024 as a small weaving stall in Namakkal, run by a mother who believed every woman deserved to feel like royalty on her own terms. Today, that belief has grown into a full boutique house.

We source directly from handloom clusters in Kanchipuram and Coimbatore, and pair centuries-old weaving techniques with contemporary silhouettes — for weddings, festivals, and everyday elegance.
